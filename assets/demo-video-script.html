🎥 Product Demo Script

The Ark AI — 5 Minutes Max | Baobab Network Application

Scene 1: Introduction 0:00 – 0:30
"Let me show you The Ark in action. This is arknode.ai — our AI platform where you pay per task with Bitcoin Lightning. No signup, no subscription. Let's walk through how a real user would experience this."
🖥️ ACTION: Open arknode.ai in browser. Show the clean chat interface.
Screen recording with voice-over. Can use phone or desktop — desktop is easier to see.
Scene 2: Free Task 0:30 – 1:30
"Every new user gets a free task to try it out. Let's say I'm a developer who needs a code review."
🖥️ ACTION: Type a prompt like: "Review this Python function for bugs and security issues: [paste a short function]"
"Watch the response — it's not just 'looks good.' We use custom prompt engineering to deliver structured, professional output. You get critical issues, performance notes, security findings, and specific fix suggestions. This is the quality of a senior engineer review."
🖥️ ACTION: Let the response stream in. Scroll through to show the structured output.
Scene 3: Payment Flow 1:30 – 2:30
"Now I want another task. This time, the system asks me to pay. Let me do something more interesting — let's generate an image."
🖥️ ACTION: Type an image generation prompt. The system should show a Lightning invoice / QR code.
"See that? A Lightning invoice for the exact amount — no more, no less. I scan this with any Lightning wallet..."
🖥️ ACTION: Pull out phone, open a Lightning wallet (Phoenix, Wallet of Satoshi, or BlueWallet), scan the QR code, and pay.
"Paid. Instantly confirmed. And here's my result."
🖥️ ACTION: Show the result appearing after payment.
Scene 4: Service Variety 2:30 – 3:30
"That was just one of 120 services. Let me show you the range."
🖥️ ACTION: Quick montage or scroll through showing different task types. Maybe type 2-3 quick examples:
Examples to show (pick 2-3):
• "Write an NDA for a freelance contractor in Kenya" (legal)
• "Analyze the SEO of nike.com" (SEO analysis)
• "Generate a Dockerfile for a Python FastAPI app" (DevOps)
• "Translate this to Swahili: [text]" (translation)
"Code generation, legal drafts, medical summaries, SEO audits, DevOps automation, content writing — all from one chat interface, all pay-per-task."
Scene 5: The Wallet System 3:30 – 4:15
"For power users, we have an in-app wallet. You top up once and tasks deduct automatically — no QR code scanning every time."
🖥️ ACTION: Show the wallet creation flow (if easy to demo) or describe it.
"And here's what makes us different from every other AI tool — we also support the L402 protocol. That means other AI agents can discover our services and pay for them automatically, machine-to-machine. We're not just a chatbot — we're AI infrastructure."
Scene 6: Closing 4:15 – 4:45
"120 services, real Lightning payments, live on mainnet, and running costs of less than twenty dollars a month. This is AI for everyone — starting with Africa. This is The Ark."
🖥️ ACTION: End on the arknode.ai homepage or the chat interface with a completed task visible.
🎬 Recording tips:
• Use a screen recorder with microphone (QuickTime on Mac: File → New Screen Recording)
• Record at 1080p if possible
• Talk at a natural pace — it's OK to be under 5 minutes
• If a task takes long to generate, you can cut/speed up the waiting part
• Have your Lightning wallet ready and funded before recording
• Do one practice run to make sure payments work smoothly
• Upload to YouTube (unlisted) or Google Drive (set sharing to "anyone with link")
• Target length: 3-5 minutes (shorter is better if you cover everything)
