🎥 1-Minute Pitch Video Script

The Ark AI — Baobab Network Application

Opening Hook 0:00 – 0:10
"What if you could use the most powerful AI tools in the world — for five cents a task — and pay with just your phone? No credit card. No subscription. No signup. That's The Ark."
📷 Look straight at camera. Confident, conversational energy. Maybe hold up your phone showing arknode.ai.
The Problem 0:10 – 0:25
"Right now, 1.4 billion people worldwide are locked out of AI. ChatGPT costs twenty dollars a month and needs a credit card — that's a non-starter for most of Africa. Developers in Lagos, Nairobi, and Accra are building the future, but they can't access the same tools as someone in San Francisco."
📷 Slight lean forward — this is the part you care about. Show the frustration.
The Solution 0:25 – 0:42
"The Ark fixes this. We built 120 AI services — code generation, legal drafts, SEO analysis, image creation, medical summaries — all pay-per-task using Bitcoin Lightning. A code review costs a hundred sats. That's less than ten cents. And Lightning works everywhere — no bank needed. We even support machine-to-machine payments, so AI agents can buy services from other AI agents automatically."
📷 This is the exciting part — let your energy build. Quick hand gestures are fine.
Traction 0:42 – 0:50
"We launched four weeks ago. We're live on mainnet, processing real payments, with 120 services running and users from multiple countries already on the platform. Our AI costs less than one cent per task — that's a 95% margin."
📷 Confident nod. These are real numbers — own them.
The Ask 0:50 – 1:00
"With Baobab's support, we'll build mobile money on-ramps, so anyone with M-Pesa can use AI instantly. We're not just building an app — we're building AI infrastructure for the next billion users. And we're starting right here in Africa."
📷 End strong. Slight pause before "right here in Africa." Small smile. Hold eye contact for 2 seconds after finishing.
🎬 Recording tips:
• Film in landscape mode, good natural lighting (face a window)
• Plain/clean background — no clutter
• Phone at eye level (stack some books)
• Practice 2-3 times before recording — aim for natural, not memorized
• It's OK to paraphrase — the vibe matters more than exact words
• Total target: 55-60 seconds (leave a tiny buffer)
• Upload to YouTube (unlisted) or Google Drive and share the link
