Initial Policy Questionnaire

Purpose & Mission

Please describe your organization's mission, if applicable.

The Ark AI is a privacy-first artificial intelligence platform built on the Bitcoin Lightning Network. Our mission is to democratize access to advanced AI tools for people worldwide — particularly in Africa and other underserved regions — without requiring personal data, email addresses, accounts, or subscriptions. We believe that access to information and computational tools is a fundamental enabler of human rights, and that financial privacy is essential to individual freedom. By accepting only Bitcoin Lightning payments (starting from 50 satoshis) and collecting zero user data, The Ark ensures that journalists, activists, researchers, and ordinary citizens in restrictive environments can access over 120 AI-powered services — including research, translation, legal document generation, content creation, and code development — without fear of surveillance, censorship, or financial exclusion.

If you are an individual, please describe your work to promote human rights and liberal democracy.

As an independent developer based in East Africa, I have witnessed firsthand the barriers that prevent millions of people from accessing the digital tools that those in democratic, wealthy nations take for granted. Surveillance-based business models, subscription paywalls requiring international credit cards, and mandatory identity verification exclude vast populations from participating in the AI revolution. My work with The Ark is driven by the conviction that privacy is not a luxury but a right, and that permissionless, censorship-resistant technology — specifically Bitcoin — is the most powerful tool available to protect that right. I build technology that serves people who cannot safely identify themselves online: journalists in hybrid-authoritarian states, activists under surveillance, small business owners excluded from the global financial system, and students who simply cannot afford a $20/month AI subscription.

Please describe your proposed project, specifically highlighting the ways in which it aligns with HRF's Mission Statement and how it will further the goal of promoting human rights and liberal democracy.

The Ark AI directly advances HRF's mission in the following ways:

1. Privacy as a Human Right: The Ark requires no signup, no email, no phone number, and no identity verification of any kind. There are no accounts, no cookies, and no tracking. This design is intentional — it ensures that users in closed or hybrid-authoritarian societies (as classified in HRF's Political Regime Classifications) can safely access AI tools without creating a digital trail that could be used against them by state actors.

2. Censorship-Resistant Access: By building on Bitcoin's Lightning Network, The Ark enables instant, borderless, permissionless payments that cannot be blocked by governments or financial intermediaries. Users in countries with capital controls, sanctioned banking systems, or authoritarian financial surveillance can pay for AI services without going through any traditional financial institution.

3. Democratizing AI for the Next Billion: Our pricing starts at 50 satoshis (fractions of a cent), making advanced AI accessible to people in low-income regions across Africa, Asia, and Latin America. We offer 5 free prompts to every user with no strings attached. Our platform is specifically designed for mobile-first, low-bandwidth environments common across the African continent.

4. Empowering Civil Society: The Ark's 120+ services include tools critical to human rights work: legal document drafting (contracts, NDAs, privacy policies), research and analysis, translation across dozens of languages, content creation for advocacy, security scanning for activists' code, and document summarization. These tools empower individuals and small organizations that cannot afford enterprise AI subscriptions.

5. Open Protocol (L402): The Ark supports the L402 machine-to-machine payment protocol, enabling other developers and organizations to build privacy-preserving AI applications on top of our infrastructure — multiplying the impact beyond our direct users.

With HRF's support, we plan to expand our infrastructure, add more languages relevant to communities in closed societies, improve our mobile experience for low-bandwidth regions, and develop dedicated toolkits for journalists and human rights organizations operating in restrictive environments.

HRF Policy

Do you or your organization have in place a written policy requiring the review of potential partners, employees, or contractors for any potential support of authoritarian or partially-authoritarian regimes? Please explain.

The Ark is currently an early-stage, independently operated project. While we do not yet have a formal written policy, our foundational architecture is itself an anti-authoritarian statement: we deliberately chose to build on Bitcoin rather than traditional payment systems precisely because Bitcoin cannot be co-opted by authoritarian regimes. We do not partner with, accept funding from, or integrate services from any entity associated with authoritarian governments. As we grow, we commit to formalizing this principle into a written policy that requires due diligence on all partners, contractors, and collaborators to ensure alignment with the values of human rights, privacy, and liberal democracy.

Have you, your organization, or any individuals involved with your organization, including officers, directors, or employees, ever taken part in a violent demonstration or advocated for the use of violence? Please explain.

No. Neither I nor any individuals involved with The Ark have ever participated in violent demonstrations or advocated for the use of violence. Our approach to advancing human rights is entirely through technology: building tools that empower individuals peacefully and protect their privacy and autonomy.

Does your organization have in place a written policy requiring the equal treatment of all people, regardless of race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, political opinion or other status? Please explain.

The Ark's architecture enforces equal treatment by design. Because we collect no personal data whatsoever — no names, no demographics, no accounts — it is technically impossible for our system to discriminate against any user on any basis. Every person who visits our platform receives the same experience, the same pricing, and the same 5 free prompts regardless of who they are, where they are, or what they believe. As we formalize our organizational structure, we commit to adopting a written equal treatment policy that reflects these values explicitly.

Do any individuals involved with your organization, including officers, directors, or employees, serve as directors, officers, board members, or advisers of HRF? If yes, please explain.

No. No individuals involved with The Ark serve in any capacity with HRF.
